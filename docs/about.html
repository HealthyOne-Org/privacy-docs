About Us

Hardaway Labs is a software development company focused on creating innovative health and wellness applications. We are committed to helping people live healthier lives through technology.

Founded with a mission to make nutrition tracking simple and accessible, we leverage artificial intelligence to provide accurate, personalized health insights.

Our Products

HealthyOne

An AI-powered nutrition tracking app that makes meal logging effortless. Features include:

  • Voice-based meal logging
  • Photo recognition for food identification
  • Comprehensive nutrition tracking (calories, macros, vitamins, minerals)
  • Recipe discovery and meal planning
  • Progress tracking and goal setting

  • Accuracy - We use USDA databases and AI to provide reliable nutrition information.
  • Accessibility - Making health tracking available to everyone.
  • Transparency - Clear communication about how we handle your data.
