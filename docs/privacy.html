Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

HealthyOne ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

  • Account Information: Email address when you create an account
  • Health Data: Meals logged, nutrition information, and health metrics you choose to share
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and app version

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve the HealthyOne service
  • To sync your data across devices (if you enable cloud backup)
  • To calculate personalized nutrition recommendations
  • To send important service updates

Data Storage & Security

  • Your data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption
  • Meal data is stored locally on your device by default
  • Cloud backup (optional) uses encrypted storage with Supabase

Data Sharing

We do NOT sell your personal data. We may share data only:

  • With your explicit consent (e.g., family sharing features)
  • When required by law
  • With service providers who help operate the app (under strict confidentiality)

Your Rights

  • Access your data at any time in the app
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Export your meal history
  • Opt out of cloud backup

HealthKit Integration

If you connect HealthKit:

  • We read: Active calories burned
  • We write: Dietary calories and macros from logged meals
  • This data stays on your device unless you enable cloud backup
