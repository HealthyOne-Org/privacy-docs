SMS Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 26, 2025

Overview

This SMS Privacy Policy explains how Hardaway Labs ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you opt-in to receive SMS notifications from HealthyOne.

By opting in to receive SMS messages from HealthyOne, you agree to the terms outlined in this policy.

Information We Collect

When you opt-in to SMS notifications, we collect:

  • Phone Number: Your mobile phone number to send SMS messages
  • Opt-in Consent: Record of your consent to receive messages
  • Message Preferences: Your notification settings and preferences

Types of Messages

You may receive the following types of SMS messages:

  • Meal Reminders: Reminders to log your meals
  • Nutrition Alerts: Notifications about your nutrition goals
  • Account Notifications: Important updates about your account
  • Health Insights: Personalized nutrition tips and insights

Message frequency varies based on your preferences. Standard message and data rates may apply.

How We Use Your Information

We use your phone number solely to:

  • Send SMS notifications you have opted into
  • Provide meal and nutrition reminders
  • Deliver important account updates
  • Send health-related tips and insights

We do NOT:

  • Sell your phone number to third parties
  • Share your phone number for marketing purposes
  • Send unsolicited promotional messages
  • Use your phone number for any purpose other than stated above

Data Protection

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your phone number and messaging data:

  • Encrypted storage of phone numbers
  • Secure transmission via Twilio's platform
  • Limited access to personal data
  • Regular security audits

Opt-Out Instructions

To stop receiving SMS messages:

  • Reply STOP to any message to unsubscribe
  • Text STOP to our number at any time
  • Update your preferences in the HealthyOne app settings
  • Contact us at support@healthyoneapp.com

After opting out, you will receive one final confirmation message. You can opt back in at any time through the app.

Help and Support

For help or questions about SMS messages:

  • Reply HELP to any message for assistance
  • Email us at support@healthyoneapp.com

Third-Party Services

We use Twilio to send SMS messages. Twilio is a trusted telecommunications provider that processes messages on our behalf. Twilio's privacy policy can be found at twilio.com/legal/privacy.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this SMS Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this SMS Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • Email: support@healthyoneapp.com
  • Company: Hardaway Labs
