SMS Opt-In Process

How users consent to receive SMS from HealthyOne

Overview

HealthyOne provides optional SMS notifications for meal reminders and nutrition tips. Users must explicitly opt-in to receive SMS messages. This page documents our SMS consent process.

Key Points:

  • SMS is completely optional - the app works fully without it
  • Users must explicitly enable SMS in app settings
  • Clear disclosure is shown before enabling
  • Users can opt-out anytime by replying STOP or in app settings

SMS Opt-In Flow

1

User Opens Settings

From the app's main menu, users navigate to Settings > Notifications

2

SMS Toggle is OFF by Default

The SMS reminders option is disabled by default. Users must actively choose to enable it.

3

Clear Disclosure Shown

When tapping the toggle, users see a disclosure explaining what messages they'll receive and how to opt-out.

4

User Confirms Consent

User must tap "Enable SMS" to confirm. The toggle only turns on after explicit confirmation.

In-App Consent Screen

Consent Text Shown to Users

SMS Consent Disclosure

"By enabling SMS reminders, you agree to receive text messages from HealthyOne at the phone number provided. Messages may include:

  • Meal logging reminders
  • Nutrition goal updates
  • Health tips and insights

Message frequency varies based on your preferences. Standard message and data rates may apply.

To opt-out: Reply STOP to any message or disable SMS in app settings.

For help: Reply HELP or email hardawayhealth25@gmail.com"

Message Types

Users who opt-in may receive these types of messages:

  • Meal Reminders: "Time to log your lunch! Open HealthyOne to track your meal."
  • Goal Updates: "You've hit 80% of your protein goal today! Keep it up."
  • Weekly Summary: "Your weekly nutrition summary is ready. Check your progress in the app."

Message frequency: 1-3 messages per day maximum, based on user preferences.

Opt-Out Methods

Users can stop receiving SMS messages at any time:

  • Reply STOP to any message
  • In-App: Settings > Notifications > Disable SMS Reminders
  • Email: Contact hardawayhealth25@gmail.com

After opting out, users receive one final confirmation message and no further SMS will be sent.

Data Protection

  • Phone numbers are stored securely and encrypted
  • Phone numbers are never sold or shared with third parties
  • SMS is delivered via Twilio's secure platform
  • Users can request deletion of their phone number at any time
